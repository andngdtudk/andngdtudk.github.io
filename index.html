DANG VIET ANH NGUYEN

Dang Viet Anh (Andrew) Nguyen is a PhD student in the Machine Learning for Smart Mobility research group at the Division for Transportation Science, Technical University of Denmark (DTU). His research lies at the intersection of Operations Research and Machine Learning, with a particular focus on applying Reinforcement Learning to control and optimize large-scale, complex systems.

Before joining DTU, he worked as a Research Engineer in Intelligent Systems and Optimization at the School of Computing and Information Systems, Singapore Management University. He holds a Master's degree in Supply Chain and Logistics from Nanyang Technological University, Singapore.
